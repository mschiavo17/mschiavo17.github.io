Early Life

When I was younger, I had no idea that shoes even mattered. In my earliest ages, i had those shoes, but of course I didn't know what they were or what they were about. My mom bought them for me. In 7th grade, I started noticing shoes. My friends had cool shoes that were made by basketball players, like Michael Jordan and LeBron James. I got my first pair of LeBron's in 7th grade. This brought me to my interests in Jordan's and other players' shoes.

Present Life

I am now 16 years old, and I have about 14 pairs of shoes that range from $90-$200. They range from Jordan's, Nike's and more. I continue to collect shoes because I like spending my money.
